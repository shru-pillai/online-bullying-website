What is Online Bullying?

Online bullying or cyberbullying is the use of digital platforms such as social media, messaging apps, and websites to harass, threaten, or embarrass someone. It includes spreading rumors, sending hateful messages, or sharing personal photos without permission.

Why Does It Happen?

Online bullying often happens due to peer pressure, jealousy, revenge, or boredom. The anonymity of the internet gives some people courage to act cruelly without facing immediate consequences.
